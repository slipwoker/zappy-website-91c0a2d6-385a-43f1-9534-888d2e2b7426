    Welcome to Kong-Ping Thai Restaurant

    At Kong-Ping, we bring you an authentic pad thai restaurant experience that honors centuries of Thai culinary tradition. Our chefs masterfully blend traditional techniques with contemporary presentation, creating dishes that delight both the palate and the eye. Whether you're a seasoned Thai food enthusiast or discovering these flavors for the first time, our menu offers a journey through Thailand's rich gastronomic heritage.

    Located in the heart of the city, our Thai restaurant serves as a cultural bridge, offering urban food enthusiasts and cultural cuisine explorers an immersive dining experience. From our signature pad thai to lesser-known regional specialties, every dish is prepared with authentic ingredients and time-honored recipes passed down through generations. kkkjj

    Our Signature Thai Dishes

    Pad Thai Kong-Ping

    Our signature pad thai restaurant specialty features rice noodles stir-fried to perfection with tamarind sauce, fresh shrimp, tofu,
    bean sprouts, and crushed peanuts. This iconic dish represents the heart of Thai street food elevated to fine dining standards.

    Tom Yum Goong

    This aromatic hot and sour soup showcases the complexity of Thai flavors. Fragrant lemongrass, galangal, and kaffir lime leaves create a broth that's both invigorating and comforting, perfectly balanced with fresh prawns and mushrooms.

    Green Curry (Gaeng Keow Wan)

    Our thai thai restaurant green curry is a testament to authentic Thai cooking. Made from scratch with fresh green chilies, Thai basil, and coconut cream, this curry delivers layers of flavor that range from sweet to spicy, served with jasmine rice.

    Mango Sticky Rice

    A beloved Thai dessert that perfectly balances sweet and savory. Glutinous rice cooked in coconut milk is paired with fresh, ripe mango and topped with a rich coconut cream sauce, creating a memorable finish to your meal.
