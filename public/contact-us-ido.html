    Reservations

    We recommend making reservations, especially for dinner service and weekends. Walk-ins are welcome based on availability. For large groups of 7 or more, please call us directly to ensure we can accommodate your party comfortably.

    Parking & Accessibility

    Private Events & Catering

    Frequently Asked Questions

    Do you accommodate dietary restrictions?

    Yes, we offer vegetarian, vegan, and gluten-free options. Our chefs can modify most dishes to accommodate allergies and dietary preferences. Please inform your server of any restrictions when ordering.

    How spicy are your dishes?

    We prepare dishes at various spice levels. Our menu indicates spiciness, and we're happy to adjust the heat level to your preference—from mild to authentically Thai spicy.

    Do you offer takeout and delivery?

    Yes, we offer both takeout and delivery services. Call us directly or order through our preferred delivery partners. Our packaging ensures your food arrives fresh and at the proper temperature.

    What makes Kong-Ping different from other Thai restaurants?

    Our commitment to authenticity sets us apart. We use traditional recipes, import key ingredients from Thailand, and employ chefs trained in authentic Thai cooking methods. We don't compromise on quality or authenticity to appeal to different palates.
